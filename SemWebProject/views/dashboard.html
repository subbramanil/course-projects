Veteran Health Administration & Info.

The Project tries to find a relation between the medical facilities, infrastructures, services available, treatments, surgeries and the accreditation status per county. This project extracts data from the year 2008 and it determines how medical facilities supports the veterans and how their facilities fare against each other in each county

Dataset 1207 - Veterans Health Administration 2008 Hospital Report Card - Procedure Volume
No of Triples. 1813

This dataset contains the different types of surgery that are performed by the medical facility.

Dataset 1208 - Veterans Health Administration 2008 Hospital Report Card - Quality of Care - Populations
